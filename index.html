ABOUT MY HOBBY

My favourite hobby was to play boardgames like SCRABBLE but during this Pandemic,Now I love to read books.I like to read Fun-Filled Mystery Books like the Find-Outers series by Enid Blyton and Funny Comics like Tinkle.I like to read these books in my leisure time if I have any.

Now I have understood that books are little friends of people which not only help in passing time but also "OPEN A VOCABULARY BANK" for us. It gives us knowledge to us about many new words and makes one discipline as well.

People usually are lazy to read books but reading books is one of the best hobby one can develop.
